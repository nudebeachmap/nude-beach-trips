Optional Adventures

Enhance your trip with these curated local tours.
*All tours are optional extras, payable in cash (Pesos) upon arrival.

1000 MXN (55 USD) / person

Marine Safari & Sunrise

We'll get picked up at 6:30 AM. We board the boat at Playa Panteón and set out to spot dolphins, whales, and turtles. Then we'll visit three virgin beaches (La Boquilla, La Mina, Estacahuite) and go snorkeling (gear included).

Duration: 3 Hours. Return 10:00 AM. Clothing Optional on boat. Includes transport.

$1,600 MXN (89 USD) / person

Bioluminescence Lagoon

We depart at 5:00 PM for a magical night tour. We enter the lagoon to see the water glow in the dark. The water is warm (thermal springs). Swim in the stars! We'll stop for dinner (optional) on the way back.

Duration: 6.5 Hours. Return approx 11:30 PM. Clothing Optional at certain locations.

$2,500 MXN (140 USD) / person

Magical Waterfalls Hike

We leave at 6:00 AM. We'll guide you on a 20-min jungle walk to a majestic 30-meter waterfall. Swim in fresh mountain springs and explore a hidden cave jacuzzi. Includes delicious Buffet Breakfast (mole, meats, coffee).

Duration: 8 hours. Return approx 2:00 PM. Clothing Optional at falls.

$1,400 MXN (78 USD) / person

Mangroves & Turtle Release

We depart at 6:30 AM. We'll explore the mangroves by boat (non-motorized) to spot birds, iguanas, and crocodiles. We'll visit a crocodile sanctuary on an island. On the return, we help release newborn turtles into the ocean.

Duration: 4 hours. Clothing is optional for some of this trip. Return approx 10:00 AM.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to get naked? +
Nudity is never mandatory—we believe it should be a personal choice, not enforced. If you want to come along to try it but aren't sure you'll be able to, that's totally fine! Everyone's on a journey to feel comfortable in their skin. However, the answer lies in intent: If you have no intent on participating in the social nude space at all, then you won't receive an invite.
Why do you screen people? +

To ensure it is a safe place for everybody. Rather than provide ID (invasive!), we ask for a link to your social media accounts. We check to ensure you are a real person with a genuine interest in the community. Unfortunately, if you have a blank profile or no followers, you probably won't be invited.

What are the ages of people going? +

Our focus is the 18-55 demographic. There are plenty of wonderful clubs and spaces in North America and beyond for 55+, and in fact, most established clubs are dominated by that demographic. We created this space to facilitate younger people embracing their bodies. The activities, locations, and vibe are geared towards this energy.

How do you keep this a safe space? +

Safety is our priority. Every guest must agree to our Respect Policy. We vet every attendee, and anyone violating our simple rules of consent and courtesy will be asked to leave immediately.

Can I bring my family? +
Yes! We love naturist families. Note: The hotel does not have cribs, and some upper-floor rooms have low railings, so please request a ground-floor suite for safety.
Can I come alone? +

Absolutely! Our trips are designed for community. You'll meet plenty of other friendly travelers at the welcome dinner and daily activities. It's a very safe and social environment for solo travelers.

Why do you keep a 50:50 gender balance? +

To ensure we don't have 20 single people and one couple, or all families and one single person. We strive for a healthy mix of younger and older families, couples, and singles. We definitely welcome singles, but this is not a singles party.

Is this a lifestyle group? +

No. This is strictly a platonic, family-friendly naturist event. We respect all lifestyles, but this is not the place for that. You will be very disappointed if that is your intent and it does not align with our Respect Policy.

Can I get a refund if I cancel? +

Yes! If you cancel 90 days prior to the event you can get a full refund (minus card processing fees). 60 to 90 days: 50% refund of amounts paid. 30 days or less: no refund of amounts paid. We strongly recommend private travel insurance.

How do I pay for extras? +

Zipolite is a cash-based economy. Please bring Mexican Pesos for tours, extra meals, and tips. ATMs are available but can sometimes run out of cash.

How do I get there? +

Fly into Huatulco (HUX) or Puerto Escondido (PXM). We can help arrange group transport or private taxis from the airport to the hotel.
