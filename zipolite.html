Bioluminescence Lagoon

We depart daily at 5:00 PM for a magical night tour. We enter the lagoon to see the water glow in the dark. The water is warm (thermal springs). Swim in the stars! We'll stop for dinner (optional) on the way back.

Return approx 11:30 PM. Clothing Optional in parts.

Magical Waterfalls Hike

We leave at 7:00 AM. We'll guide you on a 20-min jungle walk to a majestic 30-meter waterfall. Swim in fresh mountain springs and explore a hidden cave jacuzzi. Includes delicious Buffet Breakfast (Mole, meats, coffee).

Return approx 3:00 PM. Clothing Optional at falls.

Mangroves & Turtle Release

We depart at 3:00 PM. We'll explore the mangroves by boat (non-motorized) to spot birds, iguanas, and crocodiles. We'll visit a crocodile sanctuary on an island. On the return, we help release newborn turtles into the ocean.

Return approx 7:00 PM.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is nudity mandatory? ‚ñº

Nudity is optional throughout the hotel and on the tours (though highly encouraged on the beach!). The environment is relaxed and natural. In town and at restaurants, light clothing is expected.

Can I come alone? ‚ñº

Absolutely! Our trips are designed for community. You'll meet plenty of other friendly travelers at the welcome dinner and daily activities. It's a very safe and social environment.

How do I pay for extras? ‚ñº

Zipolite is a cash-based economy. Please bring Mexican Pesos for tours, extra meals, and tips. ATMs are available but can sometimes run out of cash.
