The Venue: Buda Mar Zipolite

Ocean sounds create a peaceful backdrop for a genuine getaway experience. Located directly on the beach, you are just a 2-minute walk from the famous Zipolite Walkway.

Relax poolside or explore the gardens at this coastal retreat. Enjoy cooked-to-order breakfast (available), stay connected with free in-room WiFi, or unwind on the garden terrace after exploring hidden coves.
