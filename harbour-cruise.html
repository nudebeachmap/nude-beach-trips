A Floating Nude Beach

This is a calm, body-positive cruise for people who like nude beaches — and for newbies who are curious and want to try a clothes-free event in a relaxed setting.

Not a party boat. Not a dating event. Just summer, scenery, swimming, and a respectful crowd — hosted by Nude Beach Map.

The Experience

We’ll cruise out from Granville Island. Once we’re clear of the busy harbour — and conditions allow — the captain will give the go-ahead for clothing-optional time onboard. From there, we’ll find a secluded cove and drop anchor for swim time.

Clothing optional (never enforced) At least 2 hours swim time BYO food & drinks Respect & consent-first

Anchor and beach location depends on weather conditions. Nudity is allowed once the captain gives the ok — we anticipate that will be once we leave the busy Vancouver harbour.

FAQ

Do I have to get naked? +

We don’t believe that nudity should ever be enforced, so the answer is no.

But… why do you want to come to an event if you have no intent to participate?

The answer here lies in intent.

If you want to come along to try it but aren’t sure you’ll be able to, that’s totally fine. Come anyway. Everyone’s on a journey to feel comfortable in their skin. Some people dive right in whilst others take baby steps. We’re 95% certain within 20 minutes you’ll want to get involved.

If after your second event you haven’t participated, then usually both parties conclude that this isn’t the space for you.

If you have no intent on participating in the social nude space, then you won’t receive an invite.

Why do you screen guests? +
To ensure a genuine, respectful mix of people and keep the vibe relaxed and non-creepy. This is the key to making it feel safe for everyone — especially newbies.
Is this a party cruise? +
No. This is a social, body-positive environment — not a booze cruise, not a lifestyle event, and not a “scene.” Expect chilled music, great views, and respectful people who are there for the same reason: comfort and freedom.
What if I’m brand new to nude beaches? +
Perfect. This cruise is intentionally designed to be newbie-friendly: calm, hosted, and consent-first. You can take it at your own pace — no pressure, no weirdness.
What’s included? +
The yacht, crew, hosted vibe by Nude Beach Map, and time anchored for swimming (conditions permitting). Food and drinks are BYO — and you can use the BBQ and fridge onboard.
What should I bring? +
Towel, sunscreen, water, snacks/lunch, swim gear (if you want), a light layer for later, and good energy.
Where do we anchor? +
The exact anchor + beach location depends on conditions on the day. We’re aiming for a secluded bay with safe swimming and privacy once we’re clear of busy harbour traffic.
Can I take photos? +
Consent-first, always. No one should be photographed without permission. We’ll cover the rules clearly before we depart.
Why is this event 19+? +
Make no mistake this is a “family friendly” cruise. However the difficulties with children on a moving boat, challenging swimming surfaces and no lifeguards make it necessary to have this event as strictly 19+. It also ensures everyone is of legal drinking age.
