A Floating Nude Beach

This is a calm, body-positive cruise for people who like nude beaches and want a rare, clothes-free experience in a comfortable naturist setting.

Not a party boat. Not a dating event. Just summer, scenery, swimming, and a respectful crowd — hosted by Nude Beach Map.

What the day looks like

  • 11:00am – Departure from Granville Island

FAQ

Do I have to be nude? +

Do I have to get naked?
We don’t believe that nudity should ever be enforced, so the answer is “no”.

But… why do you want to come to an event if you have no intent to participate?

The answer here lies in intent.

If you want to come along to try it but aren’t sure you’ll be able to, then that’s totally fine — come anyway. Everyone’s on a journey to feel comfortable in their skin. Some people dive right in whilst others take baby steps.

We are 95% certain that within 20 minutes you’ll want to get involved. If after your second event you haven’t participated, then usually both parties conclude that this isn’t the space for you.

If you have no intent on participating in the social nude space, then you won’t receive an invite.

Why do you screen guests? +
To ensure a genuine, respectful mix of people. This keeps the vibe relaxed and non-creepy. Screening helps protect everyone’s comfort — especially first-timers.
Is this a party cruise? +
No. This is a social, body-positive environment — not a booze cruise, not a “scene,” and not a lifestyle event. Think calm, respectful, and summer-on-the-water.
Can I bring alcohol? +
BYO food and drinks is allowed, but everyone must follow the captain’s rules and any applicable regulations. No selling alcohol onboard. Please keep it respectful — we’re not here to get hammered.
Where do we anchor? +
The anchor and beach location depends on weather and conditions on the day. Our goal is privacy + safety. We plan for at least 2 hours of swim time, conditions permitting.
Photos / phones? +
Consent-first, always. No photos of other guests without explicit permission. If you’re taking a photo of yourself or your partner, make sure no one else is in the background. No sharing images of anyone (other than yourself/your partner) online.
What if I need to cancel? +
Your ticket terms are shown during checkout/application. As a small event with limited capacity, cancellations may be non-refundable depending on timing. We strongly recommend travel insurance.
