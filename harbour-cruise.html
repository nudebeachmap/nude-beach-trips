A Floating Nude Beach

This is a calm, body-positive harbour cruise designed for people who are curious about clothes-free spaces — whether you’re experienced or trying something like this for the first time.

Not a party boat. Not a dating event. Just summer, scenery, swimming, and a respectful, screened crowd — hosted by Nude Beach Map.

What the day looks like

  • 11:00am – Depart Granville Island
  • Scenic cruising toward calmer water (route depends on conditions)
  • Once clear of the busy harbour and when conditions allow, the captain will give the OK for clothing-optional time
  • We’ll anchor in a sheltered bay or near a quiet beach for swim time (at least 2 hours, conditions permitting)
  • 5:00pm – Return to Granville Island

Clothing-optional

Nudity is never forced — but it’s assumed you’re joining for the same reason as everyone else: to enjoy a rare, relaxed clothes-free experience.

Some people dive right in. Others ease into it. Both are completely fine.

FAQ

Do I have to be nude? +

We don’t believe nudity should ever be enforced, so the short answer is no.

That said, intent matters.

If you’re curious and want to try a clothes-free event but aren’t sure how you’ll feel, that’s totally fine — come along anyway. Everyone is on their own journey. Some people dive right in, others take baby steps.

In our experience, 95% of people are comfortably participating within 20 minutes.

If after a second event someone hasn’t participated at all, it’s usually clear to both parties that this isn’t the right space — and that’s okay.

If you have no intent to participate in a social nude space, you won’t receive an invite.

Is this good for first-timers? +
Yes — many guests are first-timers. This cruise is specifically designed to feel safe, low-pressure, and welcoming for people trying a clothes-free experience for the first time.
