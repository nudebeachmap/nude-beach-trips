FAQ

Do I have to get naked? +

We don’t believe that nudity should ever be enforced, so the answer is no.

But… why do you want to come to an event if you have no intent to participate.

What matters is your vibe and your intent. This cruise is for people who are genuinely curious about a clothes-free, respectful space — even if you’re not sure how quickly you’ll feel comfortable.

Some people strip off straight away. Others take their time. Both are totally fine.

Why do you screen guests? +
To ensure a genuine, respectful mix of people. This keeps the vibe relaxed, safe, and non-creepy — especially for first-timers.
Is this a party cruise? +
No. This is a social, body-positive environment — not a booze cruise, not a dating event, and not a “scene.”
What if I’m brand new to nude beaches? +
Perfect. This is a relaxed, hosted, newbie-friendly way to try a clothes-free event in a respectful setting. Take it at your own pace.
What’s included? +
The yacht + crew, hosted vibe by Nude Beach Map, and time anchored for swimming (conditions permitting). Food and drinks are BYO — and there’s a BBQ + fridge onboard.
What should I bring? +
A towel, sunscreen, water, snacks/lunch, a light layer for later, and anything you want for a comfortable day on the water.
Where do we anchor? +
The exact anchor + beach location depends on weather and conditions. We aim for a secluded bay with safe swimming and privacy.
Can I take photos? +
Consent-first, always. No one should be photographed without permission. We’ll cover the rules before we depart.
Why is this event 19+? +
Make no mistake this is a “family friendly” cruise. However the difficulties with children on a moving boat, challenging swimming surfaces and no lifeguards make it necessary to have this event as strictly 19+. It also ensures everyone is of legal drinking age.
