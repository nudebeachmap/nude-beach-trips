A Body-Positive Day on the Water

A relaxed summer cruise for open-minded people who want something more meaningful than the usual crowded boat day.

This is about confidence, comfort, sunshine, swimming, and being around people who understand that every body belongs.

Not a party boat. Not a dating event. Just a calm, respectful, body-positive experience hosted by people who care about creating the right vibe.

The Experience

Departing from Port Moody at 11:30am, this 4-hour cruise heads into one of the most scenic waterways near Vancouver.

We’ll cruise through calm waters, take in the scenery, and anchor in a quiet, sheltered spot for a swim. The exact location will depend on conditions on the day.

A DJ will set a relaxed summer atmosphere onboard — more easygoing background energy than full-on party mode.

The goal is simple: a comfortable, respectful space where people can unwind, feel good in their own skin, and enjoy a beautiful afternoon on the water.

You don’t need to look a certain way. You don’t need to be part of any scene. You just need to be kind, open-minded, and respectful of the people around you.

FAQ

Is this beginner-friendly? +

Yes. This is designed to feel easy, calm, and welcoming, especially for people trying something new.

Is this a party cruise? +

No. It’s social and fun, but not a wild party boat. Think relaxed music, good chats, swimming, floaties, and a respectful crowd.

Is this a dating event? +

No. This is a body-positive social cruise, not a dating event.

Can I come solo? +

Yes. Solo guests, couples, and friends are all welcome. The application helps us keep the group balanced and respectful.

Why do you ask people to apply? +

Because the group dynamic matters. A short application helps us create a comfortable, safe-feeling, and respectful environment for everyone onboard.

What kind of people usually come? +

Open-minded, respectful people who enjoy nature, swimming, good conversation, and more body-positive social spaces.

What’s the photo policy? +

Consent-first. No photos or videos of anyone else without clear permission. If you’re taking a selfie, make sure nobody is in the background.

Can I share photos online after? +

Only photos of yourself or your own group, with permission. Please do not post other guests without their clear consent.

Is BYO food and drinks allowed? +

Yes. Bring your own drinks, snacks, and floaties. We want this to feel easy and relaxed.

Where do we anchor? +

Somewhere scenic and sheltered, depending on weather and water conditions. Safety comes first.

What if the weather is bad? +

Route and anchoring are weather-dependent. If conditions don’t allow for a safe swim stop, we’ll adjust the plan and keep the day scenic and safe.

Why is this event 19+? +

Because it’s a private boat event with BYO drinks, swimming, and no lifeguards. Keeping it 19+ makes the day simpler and safer.
